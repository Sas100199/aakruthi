Introduction :

Welcome to Aakruthi, the premier technical fest organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering. Aakruthi serves as a platform to showcase innovation, technical expertise, and creative problem-solving skills. With a series of challenging events, workshops, and competitions, Aakruthi brings together students, professionals, and industry experts to explore the latest advancements in the field of electronics and communication. Our mission is to foster a culture of learning, collaboration, and technical excellence. Join us for an enriching experience, where ideas meet execution, and technology meets creativity.
